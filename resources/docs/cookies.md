This page explains what cookies Bloom uses and why.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. Bloom uses them to keep you logged in and to understand how the application is used.

## Cookies Bloom uses

### Session cookie — `laravel_session`

- **Type:** Essential
- **Duration:** Until you log out, or two weeks of inactivity
- **Purpose:** Keeps you authenticated between page loads. Bloom cannot function without this cookie.
- **Consent required:** No — this cookie is strictly necessary.

### Analytics cookies — `_pk_id`, `_pk_ses`

- **Set by:** stat.istic.net (Matomo)
- **Duration:** `_pk_id` lasts 13 months; `_pk_ses` expires at the end of your session
- **Purpose:** Track page views and events to help us improve Bloom. IP addresses are anonymised. No advertising profiles are built.
- **Consent required:** These cookies are set under legitimate interests. You can opt out at any time — see below.

## How to control cookies

You can delete or block cookies via your browser settings. Disabling the session cookie will prevent sign-in.

To opt out of Istic's Matomo analytics tracking, visit our [opt-out page](https://stat.istic.net/index.php?module=CoreAdminHome&action=optOut).
