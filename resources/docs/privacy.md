Bloom is operated by **Istic Networks Ltd.**, registered in England and Wales. Contact: [hello@istic.net](mailto:hello@istic.net) · [istic.co](https://istic.co).

## 1. Data we collect

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Name and email address** — to identify your account and send passkey recovery emails.
- **Passkey credentials** — a cryptographic public key, credential ID, and usage counter enabling passwordless sign-in. Your private key never leaves your device.
- **Session data** — IP address, browser user agent, and activity timestamps to maintain your login session.
- **Social account credentials** — your Mastodon handle and instance URL, or your Bluesky handle, and OAuth tokens used to fetch your home feed.

We also collect:

- **Analytics data** — page views and in-app events via Matomo (stat.istic.net). IP addresses are anonymised after recording. We do not build advertising profiles.

## 2. Legal basis for processing

| Data | Purpose | Legal basis |
|---|---|---|
| Name, email | Account management, passkey recovery | Contract (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Passkey credentials | Authentication | Contract (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Session data | Maintaining your login session | Contract (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Social credentials and tokens | Fetching your social feed | Contract (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Analytics | Product improvement | Legitimate interests (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) |

## 3. How we use your data

We use your data only to operate Bloom. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose unrelated to the service.

## 4. Third parties

- **Matomo** — analytics hosted on stat.istic.net, configured to anonymise IP addresses. Data does not leave our infrastructure.
- **Mastodon and Bluesky providers** — we communicate with your connected provider's API on your behalf. Credentials are stored only to maintain this connection.

## 5. Data retention

- Account data (name, email, passkeys) is held while your account is active.
- Session data is cleared on logout or after two weeks of inactivity.
- Social account credentials are deleted when you disconnect an account.
- Analytics data is retained for 13 months, then automatically deleted.

## 6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object to processing of your personal data. To exercise any right, email [hello@istic.net](mailto:hello@istic.net). We will respond within one calendar month.

## 7. Complaints

To complain about how we handle your data, contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): [ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk) · 0303 123 1113.
